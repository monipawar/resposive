Our mission

"Our Mission: To save and transform lives by raising awareness, advocating for, and facilitating organ donation. We are dedicated to educating communities, fostering partnerships, and ensuring that every individual understands the power they possess to give the gift of life. Our aim is to eradicate the organ shortage crisis, enhance medical advancements, and promote a culture of generosity and compassion in the realm of organ transplantation."

  • To raise the amount of donations pledged in India by extensive information dissemination using a variety of media and platforms, including social media, radio, television, awareness campaigns in hospitals, schools, and other institutions, as well as teaching people how to carry out the program.

  • To inform persons with organ failure of their options, to support them during their journey by providing information, advice, counseling, and financial assistance, if possible, and to stop their disease and eventual death.

  • To influence Indian public policy on organ donation through lobbying, by taking the lead in raising public awareness and working with other NGOs in this area.
